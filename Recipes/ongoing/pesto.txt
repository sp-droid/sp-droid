pesto genovese
5 servings

50 gr basil leaves
8 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
8.8 gr pine nuts (traditional) or pistachios or walnuts
2 clove of garlic
6 tbsp parmigiano reggiano
2 tbsp pecorino
small pinch of salt

Official recipe: https://www.mangiareinliguria.it/pesto-genovese/ricetta-pesto-genovese
