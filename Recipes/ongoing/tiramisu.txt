75g sugar
3 egg yolks
250g mascarpone (>40% fat)
coffee
cacao powder
savoiardi

Brew coffee and set aside

Cream:
Whip the yolks (whites are excess) with the sugar until it develops soft peaks
Incorporate the mascarpone, whip slowly then faster for 3 minutes. Cream shouldn't be pourable, but consistent

Layering on recipient:
Spread thin layer of cream on the bottom to avoid coffee leaks
Dip the ladyfingers once on the coffee, quickly, drain them vertically and place them
Add cream until the ladyfingers are hidden, repeat
Finally add cacao powder on top

8h rest

When serving, split in squares or nearby italians will get triggered
